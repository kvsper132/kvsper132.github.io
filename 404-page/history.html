2014 - Founding

Ninja Van was founded by Lai Chang Wen, Shaun Chong, and Boxian Tan with a vision to create a hassle-free delivery service in Southeast Asia.

2015 - Expansion to Malaysia

Just a year after its founding, Ninja Van expanded its operations to Malaysia, marking the beginning of its regional growth.

2016 - Expansion to Indonesia

Continuing its rapid growth, Ninja Van began operations in Indonesia, one of the largest markets in Southeast Asia.

2017 - Expansion to Thailand and Vietnam

Ninja Van further expanded its footprint in Southeast Asia by starting operations in Thailand and Vietnam.

2018 - Expansion to the Philippines

Ninja Van launched its services in the Philippines, completing its presence in six Southeast Asian countries.

2020 - Technological Innovations

Ninja Van introduced various technological innovations to enhance its delivery services, making them more reliable and efficient.

2022 - Continuing Growth

With a strong focus on customer satisfaction and innovation, Ninja Van continued to grow, solidifying its position as a leading logistics company in Southeast Asia.
